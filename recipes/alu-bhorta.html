Alu Bhorta

Description

Alu Bhorta is a traditional Bangladeshi side dish enjoyed with rice.

Ingredients:

  • Potato
  • Onion
  • Green chilli
  • Mustard oil
  • Salt

Steps:

  1. Boil the potatoes.
  2. Mash the potatoes with sliced onion and chillies.
  3. Add mustard oil and salt.
  4. Mix well.

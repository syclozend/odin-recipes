Egg Bhuna

Description

Egg Bhuna is a simple Bangladeshi dish for an affordable and easy meal.

Ingredients:

  • Eggs
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Green chilli
  • Turmaric
  • Salt
  • Oil

Steps:

  1. Slice and fry the onions.
  2. Add sliced tomatoes and other spices.
  3. Add boiled eggs.
  4. Cook for 5 mins.
